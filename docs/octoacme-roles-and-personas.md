# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing

### Role Summary
QA/Testing professionals validate quality, ensure acceptance criteria are met, and identify defects before release. They work closely with developers and product teams to maintain high quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Create and execute test plans and test cases
- Perform manual and automated testing
- Report and track defects through resolution
- Verify acceptance criteria and definition of done
- Participate in design reviews to ensure testability

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Maintain test coverage and documentation
- Support continuous improvement of testing practices

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Bug reports and test result summaries
- Test plan reviews with developers and product managers
- Release readiness assessments

---

## UI/UX Designer

### Role Summary
UI/UX Designers create user-centered designs, wireframes, and prototypes. They ensure products are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs through research and iterative design.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define information architecture and user flows
- Collaborate with developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Provide feedback on usability and accessibility standards
- Work with Product Manager to refine feature scope and requirements

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Advocate for user needs throughout the product lifecycle
- Maintain design consistency and brand standards
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction metrics

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings presentations
- Collaboration with developers on implementation details
- Feedback sessions with Product Manager and stakeholders

### Interactions
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on feature definition, user stories, and acceptance criteria; validate designs against business goals
- **Developers**: Provide design specifications, review implementations, answer design questions during development
- **QA/Testing**: Define expected user experiences for testing, review usability issues
- **Customer Success Manager**: Gather user feedback, understand pain points and feature requests
- **Data Analyst**: Review usage metrics to inform design decisions and iterations

---

## Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)

### Role Summary
Site Reliability Engineers ensure system reliability, scalability, and uptime for all deployments and production systems. They bridge development and operations through automation and proactive monitoring.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor system health, performance, and availability
- Build and maintain automation tools for deployment and recovery
- Oversee incident response and conduct root cause analysis
- Define and track Service Level Objectives (SLOs) and error budgets
- Implement disaster recovery and business continuity plans
- Collaborate on system architecture and scalability

### Goals
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents
- Automate operational tasks and eliminate toil
- Balance feature velocity with system stability

### Typical Communication
- Incident response coordination and postmortems
- Weekly ops reviews and reliability metrics reports
- Architecture discussions with developers
- Capacity planning meetings with Project Manager

### Interactions
- **Developers**: Collaborate on system design, deployment automation, and debugging production issues
- **QA/Testing**: Define performance and load testing requirements, review test environments
- **Project Manager**: Communicate reliability risks, capacity constraints, and deployment windows
- **Product Manager**: Balance feature releases with stability through error budgets
- **Data Analyst**: Share system metrics and performance data for analysis

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts provide insights and analysis on product usage, performance, and success metrics. They enable data-informed decision making through dashboards, reports, and trend analysis.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain dashboards for key product and business metrics
- Analyze usage patterns, trends, and user behavior
- Report on success metrics and OKRs
- Support Product Manager with data-driven recommendations
- Conduct A/B test analysis and experiment evaluation
- Identify opportunities for improvement through data exploration

### Goals
- Enable data-informed decision making across the organization
- Provide timely, accurate insights on product performance
- Improve data quality and accessibility
- Drive measurable business outcomes through analysis

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews with Product Manager
- Dashboard walkthroughs and training sessions
- Analysis presentations to stakeholders
- Data requirement discussions with developers

### Interactions
- **Product Manager**: Define success metrics, analyze feature performance, support prioritization decisions
- **Developers**: Specify instrumentation and data collection requirements
- **Project Manager**: Track project KPIs and report on outcomes
- **UI/UX Designer**: Provide usage data to inform design decisions
- **Customer Success Manager**: Analyze customer adoption and engagement patterns
- **SRE**: Collaborate on system performance metrics and monitoring

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Customer Success Managers represent the voice of the customer, ensuring successful product adoption and capturing feedback for continuous improvement. They bridge the gap between customers and product teams.

### Responsibilities
- Onboard new users and ensure successful adoption
- Collect, prioritize, and relay customer feedback to product teams
- Produce training materials and documentation for end users
- Monitor customer health and engagement metrics
- Escalate customer issues and advocate for resolutions
- Conduct customer check-ins and satisfaction surveys

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce time to value for new customers
- Identify and address adoption barriers
- Champion customer needs within the organization

### Typical Communication
- Weekly sync with Product Manager on customer feedback
- Customer health reports and escalations
- Training and onboarding sessions with customers
- Feedback summaries and feature requests

### Interactions
- **Product Manager**: Share customer feedback, validate feature priorities, participate in beta programs
- **Project Manager**: Coordinate on customer-impacting releases and communication plans
- **Support**: Escalate complex issues, share common customer pain points
- **Stakeholders**: Report on customer satisfaction and adoption metrics
- **UI/UX Designer**: Provide user feedback on usability and design
- **Data Analyst**: Review customer engagement and adoption metrics

---

## Compliance Lead

### Role Summary
Compliance Leads guide the team on meeting regulatory and compliance standards, including security, privacy, accessibility, and industry-specific requirements. They ensure deliverables meet legal and regulatory obligations.

### Responsibilities
- Advise on compliance risks and regulatory requirements
- Review deliverables for adherence to compliance standards
- Maintain awareness of evolving regulations and industry standards
- Train project teams on compliance requirements
- Coordinate audits and compliance assessments
- Document compliance decisions and risk acceptances

### Goals
- Ensure products meet all regulatory requirements
- Minimize compliance-related delays and rework
- Maintain compliance documentation and audit trails
- Foster a culture of compliance awareness

### Typical Communication
- Compliance checkpoint meetings during project phases
- Risk assessments and compliance reports
- Training sessions on regulatory requirements
- Audit coordination and findings review

### Interactions
- **Project Manager**: Advise on compliance requirements in project planning, review project risks
- **Product Manager**: Evaluate compliance implications of features, inform roadmap decisions
- **Developers**: Provide guidance on secure coding practices, data handling, and privacy requirements
- **QA/Testing**: Define compliance testing requirements, review test coverage
- **SRE**: Collaborate on security monitoring, incident response, and data protection measures
